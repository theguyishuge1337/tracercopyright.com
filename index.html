 MULTI-CHANNEL BRAND PROTECTION COVERAGE

Data inputs feed the Tracer Protect platform, producing actionable data insights across:
DOMAINS

Tracer gathers critical information about new domains, monitors and scores them for risks, provides real-time visibility into attacker infrastructures, and implements effective enforcement actions for dangerous domains.

SOCIAL MEDIA

Tracer uses machine vision, language processing, and image recognition to quickly find infringing posts, fake social media profiles and pages, and other common types of brand abuse on the most popular global and regional social media platforms.

APP STORES

Tracer uses machine vision, language processing, and image recognition to constantly track, analyze, and stop brand abuse across thousands of app platforms, including all major and third-party app stores.

WEBSITES

Tracer leverages machine vision, language processing, and image recognition to find infringements within website pages, forms, custom scripts, and more.

MARKETPLACES

Tracer monitors over 95% of all online marketplace traffic. Our brand protection solution uses natural language processing, world-class image recognition technology, and risk-clustering algorithms to find unauthorized sellers, gray market sellers, fake products, and partners who don’t follow program terms.

PAID SEARCH AND ADVERTISING
